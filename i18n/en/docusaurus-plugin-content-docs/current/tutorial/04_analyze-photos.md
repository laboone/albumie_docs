# Analyze Photos
Run photo analysis to find photos that include registered people.
After analysis completes, you can check photos and counts per registered person when creating albums.
Photo analysis can take time.
Upload all photos and register people before running analysis.

The analysis procedure is as follows:

1. Click **People** in the left menu.
 Confirm all people are registered.

2. Click **Analyze with Registered People** in the top-right corner.
 A dialog appears. Click **Execute** to start analysis.

 During analysis, clicking the button shows a message that analysis is in progress.

 After completion, clicking the button shows a completion message.

:::info
If you upload new photos or register new people after analysis, run photo analysis again.
:::

:::info
If photos by person do not appear in albums(People View) even after analysis is complete,
reload your browser or close the Albumie tab and access Albumie again.
:::
