# Register People
After registering people, you can check photo counts per person and who appears in each photo in your albums.
Here we explain how to register people by selecting faces from uploaded photos.
If you already have portrait photos for each person, [Bulk Person Registration](/advanced/add-people) is convenient.

The registration procedure is as follows:

1. Click **Photos** in the left menu, open a photo that contains the person you want to register.

2. Faces are shown in square frames. Click a face you want to register.
 The **Register as Person** dialog appears. Enter a name and click **Register as Person**.

 Repeat this for all people you want to register.

You can check registered people from the **People** menu on the left.
You can also [set classes](/advanced/add-class) for people.
When classes are set, you can view photos by class on the album **People View** page.

:::tip
For person registration, choose clear photos that include the full head and shoulders.
When the face occupies a larger area in the image, face recognition works more accurately and matching quality improves.
We recommend taking individual photos of each person for registration.
:::
