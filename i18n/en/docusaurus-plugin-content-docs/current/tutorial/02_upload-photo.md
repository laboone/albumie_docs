# Upload Photos
Let’s upload photos.
The upload procedure is as follows:

1. Click the **Upload** button in the top-right corner.


2. The upload dialog appears. Click **Select Files**, choose files, then click **Open**. (You can select multiple files.)
 You can also drag and drop files into the gray area in the dialog.

:::tip
Upload time depends on your network speed and file sizes.
As a starting point, try uploading around 1,000 photos at a time.
:::

3. Check the number of selected files, then click **Upload**.
4. When upload is complete, click **Close**.

You can view uploaded photos from the **Photos** menu on the left.

After uploading photos, proceed to registering people.
Once people are registered, you can check photo counts per person and who appears in each photo in your albums.
