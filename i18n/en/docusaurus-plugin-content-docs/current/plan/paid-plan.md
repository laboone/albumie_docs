# Purchase a Plan
Albumie offers a 14-day free trial with up to 20 uploaded photos.
To continue using the service after 14 days, you need to purchase a plan.

Purchase steps:

1. Click the settings icon (gear icon) in the header.


2. Click the down-arrow on the right side of the plan section.
   Your current plan and available paid plans are shown.


3. Click the checkout button for the plan you want to use.
   The payment page appears. Enter payment information and click **Pay**.


4. After payment is complete, **Current Plan** changes to the plan you purchased,
and your usage period is extended.
A receipt is sent by email.
