# Change Receipt Email Address
Receipts are sent by email after payment is completed.
You can change the destination email address with the following steps:

1. Click the settings icon (gear icon) in the header.


2. Click the down-arrow on the right side of the plan section.
   Your current plan appears. Click **Payment Settings** to open the plan details page.

3. In the email address section, click **Update Information**,
change the email address, then click **Save**.
