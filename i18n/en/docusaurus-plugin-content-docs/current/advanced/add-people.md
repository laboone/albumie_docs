# Bulk Person Registration
If you have individual photos of people, you can upload them in bulk and register people at once.

Steps:

1. Click **People** in the left menu.
2. Click **Bulk Upload People** in the top-right area.
   The bulk upload page appears.


3. Click **Select Files**, choose files to upload, then click **Open**. (Multiple selection is supported.)
   You can also drag and drop files into the gray upload area.
4. Enter each person’s name in the name field in the input field,
   then click **Upload**.
   When upload finishes, click **Done**.
   Uploaded people are added to the people list page.

:::info
After finishing all person registrations, do not forget to run [Analyze Photos](/tutorial/analyze-photos).
:::
