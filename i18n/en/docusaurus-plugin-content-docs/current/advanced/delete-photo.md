# Delete Photos
You can delete uploaded photos using the following steps:

1. Click **Photos** to open the photo list,
   then click the photo you want to delete to open its detail page.

2. Click the three-dots button in the top-right corner,
   then click **Delete** in the menu.

3. A confirmation popup appears.
   Click **Delete** to remove the photo.

You can also delete all uploaded photos.
See [Delete a Collection](create-collection.md#delete-a-collection) for details.
