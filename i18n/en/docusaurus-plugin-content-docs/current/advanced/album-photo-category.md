# Categorize Album Photos by Event
Using the categorization feature, you can group album photos by events such as sports day or field trip.
For each category, you can quickly check photo counts and photo lists.

## How to Set Categories
When categorizing by event, it is convenient to search by shooting date and set categories in bulk.
Here we show how to bulk-set categories by date search.

Steps:

1. From the album list page, click the album you want to categorize.
2. Click the **︙** button in the top-right area, then click **Edit Album**.
   This switches to album edit mode.


3. Hover over a photo to check its shooting date/time.
   Set that date range in **Start Date** and **End Date**, then click **Search**.
   Photos taken within the specified date are shown.

4. Click **Select All** to select photos.
   Selected photos are highlighted in blue.
   You can click photos to toggle selection on/off.


5. Click **Categorize Selected Photos** in the top-right area.
   A category input popup appears. Enter a category and click **Set**.
   This applies the category to selected photos.


7. Categorized photos can be checked from the category list under the album name.
   Numbers indicate photo counts in each category.
   Click a category to see its photos.
   Click the selected category again to clear selection.
   Before checking other categories, clear **Start Date** and **End Date** and click **Search** to reset filters.
   (If not reset, date filtering remains active inside the category.)

8. After finishing categorization, click **Done** in the top-right area.
   To remove a category, select photos with that category, open the category popup,
   leave the category field empty, and click **Set**.
